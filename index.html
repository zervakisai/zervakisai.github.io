~/research/flare.md   ·   MDPI · Drones · 2026

FLARE — Risk-Aware UAV Path-Planning for Disaster Response

Published in Drones (MDPI), 2026  ·  benchmark & framework contribution

  • [01] High-fidelity benchmark for evaluating UAV path-planning in dynamic disaster scenarios (wildfires, structural collapses) using OpenStreetMap data from real-world events in Greece.
  • [02] "Ranking Inversion" discovery — statistically proved that planners optimised for survival often fail mission-critical objectives due to excessive risk aversion. Key insight for autonomous emergency response.
  • [03] Risk-Aware Control Framework — introduced a configurable risk coefficient (ρ) balancing UAV safety vs. time-critical delivery value, giving operators a principled dial.
  • [04] Rigorous validation — 450+ experimental episodes in a Gymnasium-compatible environment, with Friedman tests and Shapley attribution for interpretable result analysis.
» Why this matters for you: The same risk-aware framework generalises to any autonomous system facing a safety/value trade-off — last-mile delivery, search-and-rescue, inspection drones, even on-orbit servicing.
